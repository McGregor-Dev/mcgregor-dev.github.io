About Me

My name is Patrick Pereira, SF based photographer and photo editor.

I got interested in photography in High School, and I've turned a hobby into a side-business that I absolutely love. Any and every chance I have, I'm out looking for something new that I can add to the collection.

Aside from that, I'm also a High School P.E. teacher. High School had a big impact on my life, and physical education is something that I think we need more of.

I hope to have a positive impact on those that I teach, as well as those that I work with. It's also an incredibly rewarding experience, and I'm so thankful for everything that I've been able to be a part of.
